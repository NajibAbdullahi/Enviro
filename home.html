DATA ANALYSIS AND CURATIONDATA-DRIVEN ENVIRONMENTAL EVALUATING APPLICATION

WThe environment is a critical factor in our ability to sustain livelihood. Climate change has been a ticking time-bomb and for that reason, it is important to understand how the environment has been changing to provide a basis for action. Malaysia as a whole has seen extensive developments and has risen above expectations, but does the development come at a cost for the environment and for future generations? HOW DO WE DO IT?
