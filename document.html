USER GUIDE

Our app include 5 main pages, including Home, Country View, State Explorer, State Comparer and Our Team.

For Home, we will clearly show the current issues faced by Malaysia. Then, we will provide the beneficiary groups who need our product in order to grab our users. Then, we will show the importance and reliability of our data product.

For the country view, the time series data is presented in graph in order to visualize the data. The users can see the top figures and time series environmental data of Malaysia, including land area, carbon dioxide and greenhouse gas emissions.

For the state explorer, the users can apply Enviro's interactive maps to identify more environmental data in interested state.. The users only require to explore the states by clicking on the map. Clicking on any one of them can get more detailed information. Selected environmental factors are first shown for the year ended 2018. More historical data is shown in plots below.

For the state comparer, the users can compare the environmental data by entering the respective states. Enter the name of two states and click the button to compare two sample locations or enter search addresses of their own.

For our team, the users can identify the product developer which is Enviro team which includes Kelvin, Najib, Xianxin, YangFan and Wei Hung. We will also provided our emails for further communication.

WHO ARE WE? ENVIRO

We are a group of Master candidates in University of Malaya who are interested in Malaysian environmental issues. This website, the data and the model are the result of the capstone project of our team “Enviro”.

WHAT DO WE DO? DATA-DRIVEN ENVIRONMENTAL EVALUATING APPLICATION

The environment is a critical factor in our ability to sustain livelihood. Climate change has been a ticking time-bomb and for that reason, it is important to understand how the environment has been changing to provide a basis for action. Malaysia as a whole has seen extensive developments and has risen above expectations, but does the development come at a cost for the environment and for future generations? We are here to tell you!

HOW DO WE DO IT? DATA ANALYSIS AND CURATION

We analysed and curated data from Department of Statistics Malaysia and the World Bank Group for a period of 15 years from 2004 to 2018. We are here to present to you the most reliable data from the world.
